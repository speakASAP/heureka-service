## Level 2: Feature validation

## Level 3: Subsystem validation

Interfaces, data ownership, failure modes, and operational evidence.

## Level 4: System validation

Feed correctness, integrations, deployment evidence, and business constraints.

## Level 5: Vision validation

Deterministic, valid, public-safe Heureka feed generation.
